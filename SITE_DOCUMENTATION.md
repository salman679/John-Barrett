## Brand Identity

### Visual Identity

**Color System**

- **Primary Navy** (#0D2036): Authority, trust, professionalism
- **Royal Blue** (#134EAD): Action, confidence, transformation
- **Gold Accent** (#CBA34E): Premium, excellence, achievement
- **Soft White** (#F7F9FC): Clarity, space, sophistication
- **Neutral Gray** (#E6E8EB): Balance, support

**Typography**

- **Display**: Sora - Modern, professional, distinctive
- **Body**: Inter - Highly readable, clean, versatile

**Design Principles**

1. **Minimalism**: Clean layouts with ample white space
2. **Premium Feel**: Subtle gradients, soft shadows, elegant cards
3. **Trust Signals**: Prominent credentials, testimonials, stats
4. **Clear Hierarchy**: Bold headings, scannable content
5. **Human Touch**: Personal photos, authentic testimonials

### Voice & Tone

**Brand Voice**

- **Authoritative** yet approachable
- **Inspiring** without being preachy
- **Professional** but warm
- **Action-oriented** and results-focused

**Writing Style**

- Use active voice
- Short, punchy sentences
- Concrete examples over abstract concepts
- "You" language (speak directly to reader)
- Avoid jargon; explain when necessary

## Copy Guidelines

### Headlines

**Formula**: [Action Verb] + [Desired Outcome] + [Time/Method]

Examples:

- "Become the Leader Your Future Demands"
- "Transform Your Team in 90 Days"
- "Accelerate Your Leadership Journey"

**Rules**:

- Keep under 10 words
- Use power words (transform, accelerate, elevate, unleash)
- Make it about the reader, not you
- Be specific when possible

### Subheadlines

**Purpose**: Expand on headline, add credibility

Examples:

- "Proven frameworks used by Fortune 500 executives"
- "20+ years of expertise helping leaders reach their potential"

**Rules**:

- 15-25 words
- Add specificity (numbers, timeframes, proof)
- Bridge to body copy

### Body Copy

**Best Practices**:

- Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences max)
- One idea per paragraph
- Use bullet points for lists
- Bold key phrases
- Include specific examples

### CTAs

**Primary CTAs**:

- "Book Your Leadership Strategy Call"
- "Start Your Transformation"
- "Schedule Free Consultation"

**Secondary CTAs**:

- "Learn More"
- "Download Free Guide"
- "Watch Success Stories"

**Rules**:

- Action-oriented verbs
- Benefit-focused
- Create urgency (when appropriate)
- Remove friction ("Free", "No obligation")
